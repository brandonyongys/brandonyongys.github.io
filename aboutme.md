Hello! Welcome to my Github. 

This is just a platform for me to share whatever mini project and thoughts I have throughout my lifelong journey. It is not so much as showcasing a perfectly executed data science project from problem definition, data collection to model development.

I am currently a data scientist for Singapore General Hospital, working on projects that would ultimately improve the outcome as well as the patient's care. Prior to joining SGH, I was working as a project officer at Nanyang Technological University (NTU) for two years. My research primarily dealt with application of Generative Adversarial Network in the finance industry. 

I have a Bachelor degree in Chemical Engineering from NTU. Now I have graduated with a Master of Science in Statistics in 2021 from National University of Singapore.

Side note: I would like to say thank you to [Dean](https://deanattali.com/) for this wonderful layout.
